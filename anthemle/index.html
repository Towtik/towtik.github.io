🌍 How to Play

1. Press the Play button to listen to the mystery anthem. You start with 1 second of audio unlocked.

2. Type and pick a country from the auto-suggest search bar.

3. Match attributes to narrow down your choices:

  • Green tile means it matches the target.
  • Red tile means it does not match.
  • 🔺 Larger / 🔻 Smaller arrows indicate the target country's population or land area comparison.

4. Each wrong guess unlocks longer intervals of the anthem (up to 30s max!).
